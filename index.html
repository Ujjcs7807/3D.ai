How 3D.AI Works

From imagination to interactive 3D world in 4 simple steps.

STEP 01
💬
Describe Your World

Type anything — "cyberpunk city at night" or "peaceful forest village" — and our AI understands your vision instantly.

STEP 02
🧠
AI Builds in 3D

Our neural engine generates full 3D scenes, objects, lighting, and textures — all automatically in under 2 minutes.

STEP 03
🌐
Enter & Explore

Walk inside your creation via browser or VR headset. Interact, modify, and customize every detail in real-time.

STEP 04
📤
Export & Share

Export as GLB, FBX or OBJ. Embed in your site or share with one link to anyone worldwide — instantly.
